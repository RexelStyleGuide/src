#colors

Usage:

IMPORTANT NOTE: DO NOT use color classes with JavaScript. Colors are for style purposes only.
CSS: For text color, use .rx-%colorName%, where %colorName% is the desired color (examples below). For background color, use .rx-%colorName%-bg

Indicator Text and Icon Colors

These Colors are used only when we need to indicate something of importance to the customer about our services or products. etc.

.rx-red-bg
#d95553 .rx-yellow-bg
#efad58 .rx-green-bg
#007b34 .rx-blue-bg
#066cb6

  • RED indicator text is #d95553 with a class of .rx-red
  • YELLOW indicator text is #efad58 with a class of .rx-yellow
  • GREEN indicator text is #007b34 with a class of .rx-green
  • BLUE indicator text is #066cb6 with a class of .rx-blue

Our four Indicator Icons have these colors by default. Go To Indicator Icons
